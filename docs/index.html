Abstract

Developer documentation and API & SDK references for implementing Vouch’s Employee Identity Origination & Recognition Platform. Recognize the human behind every device and interaction - tracing identity back to its origin in the people you trust and the organizations endorsing them to act on their behalf.

Overview

The traditional identity management systems are fraught with vulnerabilities, inefficiencies, and security risks that compromise both individual and organizational security. Vouch Identity offers a secure, decentralized solution to identity onboarding, authentication, and recovery. By leveraging cryptographic techniques and team-based decision-making, Vouch Identity eliminates single points of failure, enhances data integrity, and fosters a more seamless user experience. This documentation explores the goals, problems, solutions, and architecture of Vouch Identity and how it addresses critical problems in existing systems. It will guide you through various user flows and will show you how to use Vouch Identity to build solid and secure applications with a more secure, scalable, and user friendly approach to identity.

Introduction​

Identity management is a cornerstone of digital security, yet traditional identity systems are vulnerable to a host of attacks, including password breaches, social engineering, and impersonation. Current centralized password based systems can force organizations to operate within the walled garden of a specific identity convenience provider making it impossible to interoperate across identity boundaries. These providers not only remove governance of employees and customers away from your enterprise, but they also force access and recovery processes to work using their rules, not yours.

Vouch Identity not only addresses the weaknesses of current identity systems, but it also delivers a cryptographically secure, decentralized approach to identity on-boarding, authentication, entitlements, and recovery in a way that allows for continuity of identity with provable interactions for the vouched representatives that are on-boarded within the system. This documentation outlines the challenges of current identity systems and how Vouch Identity provides a solution that not only enhances security, reduces administrative burden, and improves user experience across the digital landscape.

Goals of Vouch Identity​

Vouched Representative: Enable organizations to endorse people with roles and entitlements that can be recognized when they act on their behalf. Continuity of Identity: Ensure the same biometric human is behind each interaction across devices, platforms, and locations to foster integrity across the system with continuous identity management and proof of provenance. Provable Interactions with Immutable Logging of Transactions: Log all transactions and interactions with identities to an immutable, capture-only data store, ensuring that every action is traceable.

Problems with Traditional Identity Systems​

Representatives in traditional identity systems use passwords/tokens/temporary credentials with all of the vulnerabilities of synthetic identities. During the enrollment process, traditional identity systems rely on passwords, tokens, and temporary credentials delivered over insecure channels, which are susceptible to being intercepted. Users often rely on weak or reused passwords, making systems vulnerable to credential stuffing, brute-force attacks, and phishing. Mitigations such as Multi-factor authentication (MFA) often introduces security gaps, such as SIM swap attacks, and can create poor user experience and fatigue.

Recovery of these synthetic identities also has problems. These include privacy concerns around centralized recovery processes, possible impersonation and phishing attacks, and social engineering, exploiting the human element of some verification systems.

In addition to the shortcomings of synthetic identities and centralized recovery schemes, traditional identity systems may rely on walled gardens which surrenders governance of users away from your enterprise to the centralized and proprietary walled garden of the identity provider. This limits the ability to recognize identities and validate endorsements across siloed internal teams, organizational boundaries, and international networks.

Finally, traditional identity systems may not be able to prove continuity of identity and provide immutable audit trails of all transactions.

Vouch Identity Solution​

Vouch Identity provides a revolutionary shift from traditional identity management by focusing on the human element and decentralizing key processes. Vouch Identity transforms how identities are on-boarded, verified, and recovered, offering a solution that scales across mobile apps, web services, and enterprise-level systems. Vouch Identity does this by adhering to the following:

  • In-person recognition and on-boarding by a trusted member of organization;
  • No temporary passwords for reduced risk;
  • Decentralized secure recovery with team voting;
  • Authority checks that are integral and always verified;
  • Smooth user experiences with no centralized IT involvement;
  • Immutable logging of all transactions and interactions.

Most enterprises have significant investment existing identity infrastructure. With Vouch Identity, you can easily integrate with mobile apps, web services and enterprise IAM SSO systems using industry standards (SAML2.0/OIDC) and Vouch Identity provided tech (SaaS, SDKs). The enterprise IAM delegates the initial access to the Vouch IDP - removing passwords and other temporary credentials from your enterprise. The rest of the SSO infrastructure will continue to work as it does today, only more secure with less hassel for your user base.

Vouch Identity Provided Tech​

Vouch Identity SDK: The Vouch Identity SDK can be included in an enterprise apps to handle user on-boarding and access to services. The Vouch Identity SDK can be used in native applications or cross platform schemes such as React Native or Flutter. The Vouch Identity SDK takes care of the following:

  • Channel Communications (BLE, WiFi, UWB)
  • Key Management System
  • Authenticator Lib

Vouch Identity Backend Service: The Vouch Identity Backend Service is a SaaS offering that is setup for the enterprise to manage and take care of the following:

  • Vouch Identity SSO
  • Integration Points SAML 2.0 / OIDC
  • Integration Points SCIM
  • Authenticator Lib
  • Transactional System
  • Immutable Data
  • Audit Logs
  • eKYC/mKYC
  • Key Management System
  • Data Pipeline / Sync
  • Verifiable Chain of Provenance
  • Secure R/O API for External Parties to Verify
